DevOps is not a tool.
DevOps is not a process.
DevOps is not a certification process.

DevOps is a philosophy.
DevOps is the thing you go back to each time you are faced with the question, "What do I do next?"

The thinking that drives me is:

DevOps is the way in which a technology organization embeds itself in a business to the benefit of that business.

That is where my DevOps journey starts.
